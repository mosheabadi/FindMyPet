About Us

Our mission at FindMyPet is to help minimize the amount of pets that end up on the streets because they got lost from their owners. We believe that the system we've created will help owners to spread the word about their lost pets, but also that it will be an efficient way for people who find the lost pets to contact the owners and help get the animals back to their homes.

All you have to do to be a part of FindMyPet is register, then you can post about any pets you lose or help other people find theirs!
